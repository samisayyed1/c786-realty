Common questions

Answers, before you ask.

If you don't see your question here, send it our way — we reply within 24 hours.

Can a foreigner buy property in Dubai?

Yes — without restriction in designated freehold areas, which cover virtually every address you'd actually want: Palm Jumeirah, Downtown, Marina, Business Bay, Emirates Hills, Jumeirah Bay, and dozens more. We handle the full DLD title transfer, developer NOC, and post-purchase paperwork.

What are the total costs on top of the property price?

Roughly 7–8% on top of a ready property: 4% DLD transfer fee, 2% agency commission, plus trustee, NOC, and mortgage-registration fees if applicable. You get a fully itemized cost sheet before signing anything.

Do you work with off-plan and off-market deals?

Actively. We hold direct allocations across Danube Properties — including Shahrukhz, Breez, Fashionz, Sportz and Greenz — alongside Emaar, Damac, Sobha, Nakheel and Meraas, with launch-day units that never reach the public release. Our off-market secondary inventory is shared only after the first consultation.

Can I get a UAE residency visa through property?

Yes — AED 750k+ in ready property qualifies for a 2-year investor visa. AED 2M+ qualifies for the 10-year Golden Visa, which extends to spouse, children, and parents. We outline exactly what your purchase qualifies for during the consultation.

How do you charge?

Standard market commission of 2% on the closed transaction, in line with Dubai practice. The discovery call, market briefing, and shortlist are free. You only pay if and when we close a deal you're happy with.

I'm based abroad — can you handle everything remotely?

Yes. We've closed deals with clients who never set foot in Dubai until handover day. Virtual viewings, power-of-attorney processing, remote signing, currency-efficient settlement — we run the full chain. Many of our clients come from London, Riyadh, Mumbai, Singapore, and New York.

Do you handle short-term rentals and Airbnb?

We advise on short-let strategy and connect clients with vetted, licensed holiday-home operators. For long-term rentals, we handle the entire tenant cycle — listing, viewings, Ejari, and renewals.
